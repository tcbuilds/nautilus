# MVP — [project name]

## Problem statement

What hurts, who it hurts, why now.

## Target user / use case

Who is this for? What is the specific situation in which they reach for this tool?

## Proposed solution (high-level)

The shape of what you'd build. Not the implementation — the *shape*. What does the user see, what does it do, what changes about their situation after using it.

## Success criteria

How will you know it worked? Quantitative if possible. "X users in Y days," "process Z requests per second," "shipped within N weeks."

## Constraints

- **Tech:** stack you must or can't use, integrations required.
- **Time:** budget for shipping v1.
- **Budget:** dollars, infra, paid services.
- **Other:** legal, compliance, partner dependencies.

## Open questions

Things you genuinely don't know yet. List them. Spec refinement will hammer on these.
