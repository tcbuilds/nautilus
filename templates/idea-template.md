# Idea — [working title]

Earlier-stage than the MVP template. Use this when the concept is still forming and you want to capture it before it evaporates.

## The seed

One paragraph. What's the idea? Write it like you're texting a friend — just get it out.

## Why now / why this

What changed in the world (tech, market, your own situation) that makes this idea worth doing today instead of last year or next year?

## Closest existing thing + how this is different

What already exists in the same space? Be specific — name names. How is the proposed thing meaningfully different? If it isn't, you might be looking at a feature, not a product.

## What success looks like in 90 days

Concrete picture of "this worked." Not vision-statement language — actual artifacts. "X users signed up," "Y revenue," "Z partnerships," "shipped to public," "personally using it daily."

## Biggest unknown

The single thing you most need to figure out before you can commit to building this. Naming it now saves a week of meandering later.

---

*When the answers here feel solid, graduate to `mvp-template.md` and start filling in scope, success criteria, and constraints.*
