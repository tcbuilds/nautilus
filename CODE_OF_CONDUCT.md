# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

Contributors are expected to keep discussion professional, specific, and focused on improving the project. Good-faith disagreement is welcome; harassment, personal attacks, or disclosure of private information are not.

## Scope

This applies to issues, pull requests, discussions, and any project-maintainer communication related to Nautilus.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments that violate this standard. Repeated or severe violations may result in blocked participation.

## Reporting

Report conduct concerns through the repository owner profile or private maintainer contact if available. Include links, screenshots, and context when possible.
